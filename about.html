About Us

You can be a part of our team ...

We started this website as a part of our final year mini project. We were a small group of 3, trying to explore ways as to how could we provide content online which can be accessible from any device. Since online there are many contents available by different faculties of various colleges. Our main motive was to provide students with all the study material needed for their exams at one place without any cost or fees, hence reducing efforts in getting good, quality study material from the colossal number of sources available. However, we will keep upgrading the website as when possible and would appreciate if anyone is ready to contribute to our social work. We take in all the feedbacks seriously and will try our best to improve your experience on our website.

Why Choose Us?

We provide all the necessary study material for students at one place. Students can access our content and download required material if required. We are providing courses for the final year computer engineering students as of now. We will be expanding to other courses as well.
